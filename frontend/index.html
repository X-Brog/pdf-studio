❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to use?
Yes, completely free. No sign up required, no watermarks, no limits.
Can I use this on my phone?
Yes! PDF Studio works on Android and iPhone.

1. Open the website on your phone browser
2. Tap the upload area
3. Choose Files or Gallery to pick your file
4. For multiple images — tap Gallery then long press the first image and tap the rest
5. Select your conversion and tap Convert
6. Tap Download to save to your phone
How do I convert multiple images to one PDF?
1. Click Convert Files tab
2. Click the upload area
3. Hold Ctrl and click to select multiple images at once
4. Select Image → PDF option
5. Click Convert and download your PDF
Are my files safe?
Yes. Your files are processed securely and automatically deleted from our servers after 5 minutes.
What is the maximum file size?
You can upload files up to 50MB in size.
What file types are supported?
PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT, JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF and WEBP.
